What Is NonPolar?

NonPolar is a group of 5 University of Michigan undergraduate students committed to informing everyday citizens on trends in politics in a newsletter that is accurate, consise, and digestible. Yada yada yada click here to learn more

What we Do

We created a newsletter for regular, everyday people. Our goal is to promote critical thinking and teach people the skills needed to be a well-informed individual, while making information digestible,.

How We Do It

None of our work would be possible without the Barger Leadership Institute (BLI). The BLI provides classes and other programming centered around leadership through the college of Literature, Science, and Arts and the University of Michigan. Each year, they fund a select group of projects through their Capstone Cohort to tackle complex problems. Find out more on their website here.
